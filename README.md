🌍 Global ATS Bridge: Lossless International CV Normalization & US Market Alignment
The Global ATS Bridge is a precision-engineered recruitment technology platform that normalizes international professional backgrounds into standardized, ATS-optimized US formats without losing semantic fidelity or data integrity.

🎯 Project Vision
In a globalized labor market, qualified international candidates often face "parsing rejection" due to layout complexity, linguistic drift, and non-standard grading scales. This project automates the transition from international standards to US corporate expectations for nine core languages, ensuring every candidate's story is heard by both machines and human recruiters.

🧠 Core Architecture & Technology
The platform leverages advanced AI infrastructure to handle the complex spatial and linguistic nuances of international documents:
- Gemini 3 Flash Vision (Dec 2025 Release): Features state-of-the-art multimodal reasoning for complex document analysis—including multi-column layouts and graphical elements—at real-time processing speeds.
- Automatic 9-Language Translation: Seamlessly converts CVs (Italian, Portuguese, French, Spanish, German, etc.) into professional US English with zero data loss.
- US-Standard ATS Formatting: Automatically reorders information into the "Gold Standard" reverse-chronological format, prioritizing recent experience and standard section headings.
- Deterministic Grading Engine: A rigid lookup system ensures that international academic honors (e.g., Italian 110L or Indian CGPA) are converted to US GPA equivalents with 100% mathematical accuracy.

🚀 The Accuracy & Reinforcement LoopTo achieve "Lossless" data transformation, the project employs a rigorous Reinforcement Learning and Few-Shot Learning process:
- Validated "Golden Samples": A repository of human-verified CVs serves as the definitive benchmark for the model's output.
- Few-Shot Grounding: Validated examples are injected directly into the model's context to reinforce the "Standard"—ensuring the AI follows specific rules for mirror-translation and structural reordering.
- Continuous QA Metrics: Every transformation is audited by a custom metrics engine scoring for bullet preservation, translation fidelity, and structural compliance.
